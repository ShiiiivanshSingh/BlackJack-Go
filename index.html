How to Play

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10): Face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points
  • Ace: 1 or 11 points (whichever helps)

Game Flow

  1. Choose your bet ($25 / $100 / $250), then deal
  2. You and dealer get two cards each
  3. One dealer card stays face-down
  4. Hit to take another card, Stand to keep your hand
  5. Double Down — double your bet, take exactly one more card
  6. Going over 21 is a "Bust" — you lose
  7. Dealer must hit on 16 or less, stand on 17+

Payouts

  • Win: +bet amount
  • Blackjack (natural 21): +1.5× bet
  • Push (tie): no change
  • Lose: −bet amount

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • H — Hit
  • S — Stand
  • D — Double Down
  • Escape — Close modals
